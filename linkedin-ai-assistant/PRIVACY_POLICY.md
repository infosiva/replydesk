# Privacy Policy for LinkedIn AI Assistant

**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

## Introduction

LinkedIn AI Assistant ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

**Important**: This extension is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by LinkedIn Corporation.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. **Profile Information (Stored Locally Only)**

When you configure the extension, you provide:
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location
- Years of experience
- Job title (optional)
- Skills (optional)

**Storage**: This information is stored ONLY on your device using Chrome's local storage API. We do NOT send this information to our servers.

### 2. **Analytics Data (Anonymous)**

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage statistics:
- Number of installations
- Daily active users
- Feature usage (which buttons clicked)
- Number of job applications submitted
- Extension version
- Browser type and version

**What We DON'T Collect**:
- Your name, email, or personal information
- Job titles you applied to
- LinkedIn account credentials
- Browsing history
- Any personally identifiable information (PII)

### 3. **OpenAI API Key (Optional)**

If you choose to use AI features (comments, summaries):
- You provide your own OpenAI API key
- This key is stored ONLY on your device
- We do NOT have access to your API key
- API calls go directly from your browser to OpenAI
- We do NOT intercept or store API responses

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Profile Information:
- **Purpose**: Auto-fill job application forms on LinkedIn
- **Usage**: Runs entirely in your browser, never sent to servers
- **Control**: You can delete this anytime in Settings

### Analytics Data:
- **Purpose**: Understand how users interact with the extension
- **Usage**: Improve features, fix bugs, measure growth
- **Control**: All data is anonymous and aggregated

### API Keys:
- **Purpose**: Enable AI features (optional)
- **Usage**: Direct communication between your browser and OpenAI
- **Control**: You can remove your API key anytime

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage (Chrome Sync Storage):
- Profile information
- Extension settings
- Usage statistics (application counts)
- API key (if provided)

**Where**: Synced across your Chrome browsers if you're signed into Chrome

**Security**: Protected by Chrome's built-in encryption

**Access**: Only YOU and the extension can access this data

### Our Servers:
**We do NOT store ANY of your data on our servers.**

The only exception is anonymous analytics sent to Google Analytics (Google's servers, not ours).

## Third-Party Services

### 1. **Google Analytics 4**
- **Purpose**: Anonymous usage analytics
- **Data Collected**: Installation count, feature usage, browser type
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

- **Purpose**: Generate AI comments and summaries

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### 1. **Access Your Data**
- All your data is stored locally on your device
- Go to `chrome://extensions/` → LinkedIn AI Assistant → Storage

### 2. **Delete Your Data**
To delete all extension data:
1. Right-click extension icon → "Remove from Chrome"
2. Or go to Settings → Clear all data

### 3. **Opt-Out of Analytics**
We don't currently offer an opt-out, but analytics are:
- Anonymous (no personal info)
- Aggregated
- Used only for improving the extension

### 4. **Export Your Data**
1. Go to extension Settings
2. Your profile information is displayed
3. You can copy it manually
