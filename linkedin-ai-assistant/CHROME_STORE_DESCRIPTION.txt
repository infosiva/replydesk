🚀 HOW IT WORKS

AUTO-APPLY TO JOBS:

1. Go to LinkedIn Jobs: linkedin.com/jobs/search
2. Search for any job (e.g., "Software Engineer")
3. Filter by "Easy Apply" (LinkedIn's one-click apply feature)
4. Our panel appears automatically on the right →
5. Click "Start Auto-Applying"
6. Extension opens each Easy Apply job
7. Auto-fills your information (name, email, phone, resume)
8. Answers basic questions using your profile
9. Submits applications with human-like delays (30-120 seconds)
10. Shows success notification for each application
11. Tracks progress: "Applied: 5/50 today"

✅ 100% automated job applications while you focus on interview prep!
